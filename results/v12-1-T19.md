RECOMMENDATIONS

If REJECT:
  1. **Acknowledge Impossibility Theorem**: The design must explicitly acknowledge the Green-Laffont impossibility theorem and choose which property to relax: strategyproofness or perfect budget balance. The system cannot have both simultaneously with individual rationality.
  2. **Revise Mechanism for Budget Balance**: If perfect budget balance is a hard requirement, replace the VCG mechanism. The system must then be designed to handle a budget surplus (typical for VCG) or, in rarer cases, a deficit requiring a subsidy.
